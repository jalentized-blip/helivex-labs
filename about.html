Our Story

Born in the Lab, Driven by Data

Helivex Labs was founded by a team of biochemists and researchers who were frustrated with the inconsistent quality of research materials available on the market. We understood that the integrity of scientific research is only as good as the compounds being studied.

What started as a small boutique laboratory has grown into a leading supplier of premium peptides, trusted by universities, independent researchers, and private laboratories worldwide. Our commitment remains the same: 99%+ purity, every time.

Quality Assurance

Our Rigorous Standards

Transparency isn't just a word for us; it's the core of our operation. Every compound we synthesize undergoes multiple stages of verification.

HPLC Analysis

High-Performance Liquid Chromatography is used to verify the identity and purity of every peptide sequence before it leaves our lab.

Mass Spectrometry

Mass Spec verification confirms the exact molecular weight and composition, ensuring no contaminants or synthesis errors.

Batch-Specific COAs

We provide full transparency with batch-specific Certificates of Analysis from independent third-party laboratories.

Cold-Chain Integrity

Our compounds are stored and shipped under strict temperature-controlled conditions to maintain their molecular stability.
